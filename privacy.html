Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to InstaTimestamp ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at instatimestamp.com.

1. Information We Collect

InstaTimestamp is designed to be privacy-friendly. Here is what we do and do not collect:

  • Instagram URLs you paste: These are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, log, or transmit any URLs you enter to our servers.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous data such as pages visited, browser type, and country of origin through analytics tools (such as Google Analytics). This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Email address: If you sign up for our waitlist or create an account, we collect your email address solely to contact you about the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the website
  • To send product updates if you have joined our waitlist (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can make it better

3. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your experience on our site. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you do not accept cookies, some parts of our site may not function properly.

We also use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous website usage statistics
  • Stripe — for processing payments securely (we never store your card details)

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since URL processing happens entirely in your browser, no Instagram URLs you enter are ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

InstaTimestamp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@instatimestamp.com
