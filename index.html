Instagram hides exact post times on mobile. Paste any post URL below — we decode the timestamp straight from the post ID.

Works with /p/ posts and /reel/ reels. No data is sent anywhere — everything runs in your browser.

How to use
1
Open Instagram and find the post you're curious about.
2
Tap the ⋯ menu → Copy link (or on desktop, click the post and copy the URL from your browser).
3
Paste the link above and hit Check Time. The exact date and time will appear instantly.
4
How does it work? Instagram's post shortcodes contain an encoded timestamp. We decode it locally in your browser — no API calls, no account needed.
